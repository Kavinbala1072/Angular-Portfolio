B.Sc. Computer Science

Bharathiar University

(Nandha Arts & Science College, Erode)

BSc Computer Science course is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of computer systems and software development which is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in Computer Science principles, as well as practical skills in programming, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering.

First Year:
Introduction to Programming, Basic Mathematics, Fundamentals of Computer Systems, and Introduction to Web Development.
Second Year:
Advanced Programming, Data Structures, Database Management Systems, Operating Systems, and Computer Networks.
Third Year:
Software Engineering, Electives (AI, Cybersecurity, etc.), Major Project, and Internship.
